Phishing Detection Tips

  • Check the sender's email address for legitimacy.
  • Look for generic greetings instead of personalized ones.
  • Be cautious of urgent language prompting immediate action.
  • Hover over links to see the actual URL before clicking.
  • Verify the domain name for any misspellings or inconsistencies.

About The School of Phish

The School of Phish is an interactive training application designed to help users identify and avoid phishing emails. By simulating real-world phishing scenarios, we aim to educate users on the common tactics used by cybercriminals and empower them to protect their personal information online.
